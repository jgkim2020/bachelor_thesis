placement_samplingrate_motion.csv

placement:
mounted at the end (toe) or in the middle (dorsal) of the shoe

samplingrate:
100Hz or 200Hz

motion:
1 - normal WIP (medium amplitude)
2 - leisurely WIP (small amplitude)
3 - marching WIP (large amplitude)
4 - normal WIP with rotation
5 - normal WIP with intermittent pause
